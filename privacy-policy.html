# Privacy Policy — PRIMALRIFT **Last updated: June 2026** This Privacy Policy describes how **Equiaux** ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you play PRIMALRIFT ("the Game") on Android or iOS devices. --- ## 1. Information We Collect ### Information you provide - **Player name** — entered voluntarily when you set up your profile. Used only to display your name in-game and in multiplayer sessions. ### Information collected automatically - **Advertising ID** — collected by Google AdMob to serve ads. You can reset or opt out of personalised ads in your device settings. - **Device information** — device model, OS version, and language. Used by AdMob and Photon for service compatibility. - **Gameplay data** — coins, selected car, completed levels, settings preferences. Stored **locally on your device only** and never sent to our servers. ### Information collected by third-party SDKs We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy: | Service | Purpose | |---|---| | **Google AdMob** | Displaying interstitial and rewarded ads | | **Photon Fusion (Exit Games)** | Real-time multiplayer networking | | **Google Play / Apple App Store** | In-app purchases and payment processing | We do not have access to data collected directly by these services. --- ## 2. How We Use Information - To run and improve the Game - To display relevant advertisements (AdMob) - To connect you to multiplayer sessions (Photon) - To process in-app purchases (Google Play / App Store) We do **not** sell, rent, or trade any personal information to third parties. --- ## 3. Advertising The Game displays ads powered by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to show personalised ads based on your interests. **To opt out of personalised ads:** - Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation - iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable for PRIMALRIFT Rewarded ads (Free Coins) are entirely optional — you choose when to watch them. --- ## 4. Multiplayer When playing online multiplayer your **player name** is visible to other players in the same session. No other personal data is shared. Session data is handled by Photon Fusion and is not stored by us after the session ends. --- ## 5. In-App Purchases Purchases (Remove Ads, Full Game) are processed entirely by the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. --- ## 6. Children's Privacy PRIMALRIFT is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Game, please contact us and we will delete it promptly. --- ## 7. Data Storage and Security All gameplay progress (coins, car selection, settings) is stored **locally on your device** using AES-128 encryption. We do not operate servers that store your personal gameplay data. --- ## 8. Your Rights Depending on your location you may have rights to access, correct, or delete data held about you. For data held by third-party services (AdMob, Photon), please refer to their respective privacy policies. For anything else, contact us directly. --- ## 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Game after changes constitutes acceptance. --- ## 10. Contact If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: **Email:** equiaux.business@gmail.com
