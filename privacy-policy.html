Privacy Policy

Equiaux · Last updated: 17 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Equiaux ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with our mobile games, including Fast Food Rush, PRIMALRIFT, and other titles we publish (collectively, the "Games"). By downloading or using the Games, you agree to the practices described here.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask you for your name, email address, or other directly identifying information to play. Some information is, however, collected automatically by the third-party services that power advertising, purchases, and stability, as described below.

1. Information We Collect

Information collected automatically by third-party services

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g. Google Advertising ID / AAID) and approximate usage data, used by our advertising partner to serve and measure ads.
  • Device & technical information such as device model, operating system version, language, country/region, and IP address (used in part to infer coarse location for legal compliance and ad delivery).
  • App activity & diagnostics such as crashes, performance data, and basic in-game events, used to keep the Games stable and improve them.

Purchases

  • In-app purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We receive a record that a purchase occurred (e.g. which product, and a purchase token) so we can deliver the item. We do not receive or store your full payment card details — those are handled by Google.

Information stored only on your device

  • Your game progress, settings, and purchase entitlements are saved locally on your device (in encrypted form) and are not transmitted to us. Uninstalling the Game or clearing its data removes this information.

2. Third-Party Services

The Games rely on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — policies.google.com/privacy. How Google uses data: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) — manages your consent choices.
  • Google Play Services & Google Play Billing (core functionality and purchases) — policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Firebase / Crashlytics (crash and stability reporting), where enabled — firebase.google.com/support/privacy.
  • Unity (game engine runtime) — unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.

3. How We Use Information

  • To display advertising and measure its performance.
  • To process and deliver in-app purchases.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve performance, content, and gameplay.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including consent and privacy regulations.

4. Advertising & Your Consent Choices

Our Games show ads through Google AdMob. Depending on your region and your choices, ads may be personalized (based on advertising data) or non-personalized.

  • European Economic Area, United Kingdom & Switzerland (GDPR): on first launch we present a Google-certified consent message where you can consent to, manage, or decline data processing for personalized advertising. You can change your choice at any time via the "Privacy" option in the in-game Settings menu.
  • United States (state privacy laws / CCPA): where applicable, you can opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your data for targeted advertising through the privacy message shown in the app.
  • You can also reset or limit ad personalization at the device level via your device's advertising settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Games are intended for a general audience and are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

6. Data Retention & Security

On-device game data remains until you delete it (by clearing app data or uninstalling). Data handled by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies. We use reasonable technical measures, including local encryption of save data, to help protect information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent. Because we do not collect account-based personal data directly, most such requests relate to data held by our third-party providers; you may exercise those rights through the providers listed above or by contacting us.

8. International Data Transfers

Our third-party providers may process information in countries other than your own. Those transfers are handled under the safeguards described in each provider's privacy policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Games after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
